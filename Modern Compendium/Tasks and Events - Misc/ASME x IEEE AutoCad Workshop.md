

# ASME x IEEE AutoCad Workshop
A mix between Mechanical and Electrical skills!

In this Multi Day workshop members will create and design their very own 3D printed + PCB Keychain! ASME will teach using Solidworks// or whatever is comftable for you// and IEEE will be teaching KiCAD. 

Key Challenges:
- Bare bone basics of CAD 
- Importing CAD sketches/profile to KiCad
- KiCAD to SolidWorks
- 3D printing design tolerances 
- Basic real world Circuit Design

The key principle is to show off the capability of solid works and kicad, most users are unaware ofo the ability to import dxf profiles into ki cad allowing for more precise design elements.
